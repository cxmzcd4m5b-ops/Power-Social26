- Traditional hand-fitting takes time but creates stronger joints
- Every log has a "perfect" position - you just have to find it
- The satisfaction when a log slides into place perfectly? Unmatched.

We're using techniques that have been around for hundreds of years. No power saws, no modern shortcuts. Just careful planning, hand tools, and a lot of patience.

**The Process:**
1. Source locally harvested logs
2. Remove bark and let season
3. Cut notches for interlocking
4. Fit each log individually
5. Add chinking between gaps
