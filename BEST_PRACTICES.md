## 🎯 Best Practices for Content

### Upload Quality

**✅ DO:**
- Upload well-lit, clear footage
- Show interesting moments (log fitting, techniques, progress)
- Mix wide shots and close-ups
- Capture "before and after" moments

**❌ DON'T:**
- Upload shaky, out-of-focus content
- Use extremely long videos (break into segments)
- Upload in poor lighting conditions

## 📱 Platform-Specific Tips

### TikTok Strategy
- **Length**: 15-60 seconds optimal
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds must grab attention
- **Trending Audio**: Use suggested music immediately
- **Hashtags**: Mix mega-viral + niche (e.g., #fyp + #logcabinbuilders)
- **Post Time**: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Frequency**: 1-3 times daily

### Instagram Reels Strategy
- **Length**: 30-60 seconds
- **Quality**: Higher production value than TikTok
- **Aesthetic**: Color grading matters
- **Hashtags**: 10-15 strategic tags
- **Post Time**: 11 AM, 1-2 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Frequency**: 1 per day + stories

### Facebook Strategy
- **Length**: 1-3 minutes
- **Context**: Tell the story, add personality
- **Community**: Respond to every comment
- **Groups**: Share in relevant cabin/woodworking groups
- **Post Time**: 1-4 PM
- **Frequency**: 3-5 times per week

### YouTube Shorts Strategy
- **Length**: Under 60 seconds
- **Value**: Educational, how-to content
- **Title**: Clear, searchable (e.g., "How to Notch Log Cabin Walls")
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant tags
- **Post Time**: Anytime (YouTube search-based)
- **Frequency**: Daily if possible

## 🎬 Video Editing Tips

### Essential Edits:
1. **Cut dead space** - No waiting, all action
2. **Speed up repetitive tasks** (1.5x-2x)
3. **Add text overlays** - Key info, measurements
4. **Use transitions** - Keep it moving
5. **Background music** - Match the mood
6. **Captions** - 80% watch without sound
7. **Strong ending** - CTA or satisfying completion

### Tools You Can Use:
- **CapCut** (Free, mobile/desktop)
- **iMovie** (Mac)
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Free, professional)
- **Adobe Premiere Rush** (Simple, paid)

## 🚀 Power Social Workflow

### Optimal Workflow:

1. **Capture** (On-site)
   - Record 5-10 clips per day
   - Mix wide, medium, close-up shots
   - Film before/after states

2. **Upload** (End of day/week)
   - Transfer to computer
   - Upload all to Power Social at once

3. **Generate** (Batch process)
   - Generate content for entire batch
   - Review and customize if needed

4. **Edit** (Desktop/mobile)
   - Use suggested edit tips
   - Add suggested music
   - Follow platform guidelines

5. **Schedule** (Weekly)
   - Use scheduling tools
   - Spread throughout week
   - Maintain consistency

6. **Engage** (Daily)
   - Respond to comments
   - Like and reply
   - Build community
